## Evaluation Team Setup

![Evaluation Team Section](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/evaluation_evaluation_team.png)

This section allows assigning the evaluation team before sending for DOI (Declaration of Impartiality).

### Actions Available

1. **Evaluation Dropdown**
   - Provides evaluation-related actions.

2. **Evaluation Team Button**
   - Opens the evaluation team selection modal.

---

## Evaluation Team Selection Modal

![Evaluation Team Modal](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/evaluaiton_team_select_model.png)

Configure the evaluation team:

### 1. Administrative Evaluation Member
- Select administrative evaluator(s).

### 2. Technical Evaluation Member
- Select technical evaluator(s).

### 3. Financial Evaluation Member
- Select financial evaluator(s).

### 4. Update Notes
- Add notes for team selection or updates.

### 5. Update Button
- Saves the evaluation team.

### 6. Cancel Button
- Closes the modal without saving.

**Note** - The list of members listed here are managed from **Settings > Management Teams > Evaluation**

---

## Send for DOI

![Send DOI Button](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/send_doi_request.png)

- After assigning the evaluation team, the **Send for DOI** button becomes visible.

---

## Send DOI Confirmation

![Send DOI Modal](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/send_doi_request_model.png)

- Displays the selected evaluation team.
- Click **Send DOI Request** to proceed.

**Behavior:**
- Sends email notifications to all selected evaluation members.

---

## DOI Email Notification

![DOI Email](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/doi_email.png)

- Evaluation members receive an email.
- Click **Accept Declaration of Impartiality** to proceed.

---

## DOI Approval Page

### Basic Details Tab

![DOI Basic Details](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/doi_approval_page_basic_details_tab.png)

- Displays procurement and evaluation details.

---

## DOI Tab

![DOI Tab](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/doi_approval_page_doi_tab.png)

### 1. DOI Tab
- Main section for Declaration of Impartiality.

### 2. Download DOI PDF
- Download the DOI document.

### 3. Approve Button
- Approve the DOI submission.

---

## DOI Approval Modal

![Approve Modal](/img/open_tender/evaluation/evaluation-team/approve_modal.png)

- Opens when clicking **Approve**.

### Actions

- Confirm DOI approval.
- Finalizes the evaluator’s declaration.

---

## Summary

This workflow ensures:

- Proper **evaluation team assignment**  
- Controlled **DOI request process**  
- Secure **email-based confirmation**  
- Formal **approval of impartiality declarations**  

This guarantees transparency and accountability in the evaluation process.
