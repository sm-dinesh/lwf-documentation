## Award Cancellation

The **Award Cancellation** process is used to cancel an already approved award and reassign approval to the next eligible bidder.

---

## Initiating Award Cancellation

![Award Cancel](/img/open_tender/evaluation/award_cancel/award_cancel.png)

- Clicking the **Award Cancel Button** opens a modal to start the cancellation process.

---

![Award Cancel](/img/open_tender/evaluation/award_cancel/award_cancel_model.png)

1. Award Cancel Button (triggers modal)  
2. Award Cancel Reason  
3. Submit Button

## Summary

- Award cancellation requires **reason input** and approval by the team  
- System **notifies approval members** automatically  
- Status updates show **cancellation in progress**  
- After approval, the next best-ranked bidder can be assigned for award approval
