# Open Tender Procedure (€30,000 and Above)

This page describes the steps and system requirements for conducting **Open Tenders** with a value of €30,000 or above in the Lwf eTender system.

## Tender Publication
Tenders are publicly advertised through:
- **National newspapers**
- **LWF social media channels**

A **standardized Tender Dossier template** is used to maintain consistency across publications.

<details>
<summary>More Details</summary>

All relevant tender information, deadlines, and submission guidelines are included in the Dossier template to ensure transparency and fairness in the bidding process.

</details>

## Bid Evaluation
The **Evaluation Committee** conducts the evaluation in three stages:

<details>
<summary>Evaluation Stages</summary>

1. **Administrative Evaluation** – Checks completeness and compliance of submitted documents.  
2. **Technical Evaluation** – Assesses technical capacity and conformity to specifications.  
3. **Financial Evaluation** – Reviews cost competitiveness and budget compliance.

Predefined criteria are applied consistently to ensure impartial evaluation.

</details>

## Selection and Ranking
- All bidders are **ranked from highest to lowest score**.  
- The ranking identifies the **most competitive offer** for further approval.

## Approval
- The **Evaluation Report** is submitted to the **Core Management Team** and **Procurement Committee** for review.  
- Approval is required before proceeding to award.

## Award
Once approved, the **Procurement Officer** prepares the award document:

- **Types of award documents:**
  - LPO (Local Purchase Order)  
  - Service Contract  
  - Works Contract  

### Steps for Preparing the Contract
1. Select the appropriate contract template.  
2. Insert the **contract amount**.  
3. Include the **selected provider’s details**.  
4. Adjust **applicable contractual clauses**.  
5. Submit to the **Core Management Team** for signature (usually requires **two approvals**).  
6. Share the signed contract with the **awarded contractor** for signature and return for recordkeeping.

## System Requirements
- Platform must allow staff to **choose the correct award template** based on procurement type.  

## Collaboration Requirements
- **Evaluation Committee members** can:
  - Collaborate directly in the system.  
  - Conduct assessments and share feedback.  
  - Finalize evaluation results transparently.  

This ensures **accountability, traceability, and efficiency** throughout the procurement process.
