# Open Tender

Open Tender is designed for high-value, competitive procurement processes where transparency and open competition are essential. This module typically handles procurements **€10,000 and above**, enabling multiple suppliers to bid and compete on an equal basis.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of Open Tender is to:

- Enable **transparent and competitive bidding processes**
- Allow **unrestricted participation** from qualified suppliers
- Support **detailed bid evaluation** with comprehensive criteria
- Facilitate **public procurement compliance**
- Ensure **fairness and accountability** in supplier selection
- Maintain **full auditability** of the procurement process

---

## ⚙️ Workflow Overview

The Open Tender process follows these key steps:

### 1. 📋 Tender Creation
- Tender document is prepared with detailed specifications
- Evaluation criteria and award conditions are defined
- Published on platforms for wider visibility

---

### 2. 🏢 Bid Submission
- Multiple suppliers can submit bids during the submission period
- Bids are submitted electronically with required documents
- System tracks all submissions with timestamps

---

### 3. 📊 Bid Evaluation
- Bids are opened on the specified opening date
- Evaluated against defined criteria
- Comparative analysis is performed using evaluation templates
- Technical and financial scores are calculated

---

### 4. ✅ Award & Contract
- Best-evaluated bid is selected
- Award notice is published
- Contract is issued to the selected supplier
- Supplier can sign and return documents electronically

## 📌 When to Use Open Tender

Use this module when:

- Procurement value is **€10,000 and above**
- You need **open and transparent competition**
- Suppliers are **not pre-identified**
- You have **complex evaluation requirements**
- Public procurement compliance is necessary
