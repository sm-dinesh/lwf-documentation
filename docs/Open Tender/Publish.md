# Publish Open Tender

## Overview

After creating an Open Tender, it is saved in a **Draft** state by default.  
In this state, the tender is not visible to bidders and cannot receive bids.

![Draft Tender](/img/open_tender/open_tender_draft.png)

To make the tender active and visible to the market, it must be **published**.

---

## Steps to Publish

1. Click on **Edit** to edit Tender record in **Draft** status
2. Navigate to the top of the page  
3. Click on the **Publish** button  
4. Confirm the action if a confirmation prompt appears  

---

## Accessing the Publish Button

![Draft Tender](/img/open_tender/open_tender_draft_publish.png)

At the top of the tender detail page, you will find the **Publish** button.  
This button is only available when the tender is in Draft status.

---

## After Publishing

Once the tender is published:

- ✅ Status changes from **Draft → Published**
- ✅ Tender becomes visible to all potential bidders
- ✅ Bidders can start submitting bids
- ✅ The procurement process becomes active
- ✅ Submission deadline countdown begins

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Before publishing, ensure:**

- All tender details are accurate and complete
- Dates are correct and in logical sequence
- Required tender documents are attached
- Evaluation criteria are properly configured
- Terms and conditions are clear and complete
- Publication channels are specified

⚠️ **After publishing:**

- Some fields may become **locked** from editing
- Published tenders may not be easily reverted to draft
- Deadlines and critical terms cannot be changed arbitrarily
- All changes are logged for audit purposes
- Monitor submission deadlines carefully

---

## Next Steps

After publishing your tender:

1. **[Manage Bid Invitations](./open-tender-bid-invitations)** – Send direct invitations if needed (optional)
2. **Monitor Bid Submissions** – Track incoming bids as they arrive
3. **Review Submissions** – Track bid submission status and completeness
4. **Evaluate Bids** – Use evaluation criteria to assess and score bids

---

## Key Differences from Draft

| Aspect | Draft | Published |
|---|---|---|
| Visibility | Only internal view | Available to public/bidders |
| Bid Submission | Not possible | Open for bids |
| Editing | Full flexibility | Some restrictions |
| Timeline | Can be extended | Deadline is firm |
| Status | Flexible | Committed | -->
