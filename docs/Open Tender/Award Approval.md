The **Award Approval** process is used to finalize and approve the selected bidder after evaluation is completed.

---

## Initiating Award Approval

![Award Approval](/img/open_tender/evaluation/approval/award_approval_button.png)

1. Highest Score Bidder  
2. Award Approval Button  
   - Opens a modal to add approval members  
   - At least **2 members are required**  

---

## Selecting Approval Team

![Award Approval](/img/open_tender/evaluation/approval/award_approval_member_selection_model.png)

1. Copy Evaluation Team  
   - Copies existing evaluators into the approval team  
2. Team Select Dropdown  
   - Allows selecting individual members  
3. OK Button to save  

---

## Notification Process

Once the approval team is added, the system **automatically sends notifications**.

![Award Approval](/img/open_tender/evaluation/approval/award_approval_notify_button.png)

1. Notify Button  
   - Can be used to manually send notifications

## If Approval is Rejected

If the bid is rejected, the system moves to the **second-ranked bidder**.

![Award Approval](/img/open_tender/evaluation/approval/award_approal_rejected.png)

1. Rank showing top bidder  
2. Status showing Rejected  
3. Send Rank Bid  
4. Award Approval Button for second-ranked bidder  

- Click **Award Approval Button**
- Update team if required  
- Send notification  

---

## Final Approval

Follow the same process for the next bidder until approval is completed.

![Award Approval](/img/open_tender/evaluation/approval/award_approval_approved_status_page.png)

1. Award Bid Button  
2. Cancel Award Button  

---

## Summary

- Award Approval is required to finalize the selected bidder  
- Minimum **2 approval members** must be assigned  
- Notifications are sent automatically (or manually)  
- If rejected, the process moves to the next ranked bidder  
- Once approved, the bid can be officially awarded or canceled
