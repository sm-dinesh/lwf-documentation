## Key Rules

- Scores **do not have to match** to resolve a technical dispute  
- Procurement team reviews and decides **Accept** or **Reject**  
- Editing scores is optional; the system allows final approval even with differences  

---

## Summary

Technical Disputes ensure **all technical evaluations are reviewed fairly**.  
Procurement users have **flexibility to resolve disputes without requiring exact score matching**.
