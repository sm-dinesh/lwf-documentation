## Important Rules

- All evaluators must have **identical responses**  
- Dispute is resolved **only when all responses match**  
- Evaluation **cannot proceed** until resolved  

---

## Summary

Administrative Disputes ensure that all evaluators are aligned on basic eligibility and compliance criteria.  
They must be fully resolved before moving to the next stage of evaluation.
