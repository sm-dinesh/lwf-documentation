## Access Purchase Order

![Purchase Order Button](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order.png)

- From the invitation listing page, click this button to navigate to the **Purchase Order** section.

---

## Purchase Order List Page

![Create Button](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_list.png)

- Click the **Create** button to start a new purchase order.

---

## Create Purchase Order

![Create Purchase Order Form](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_create.png)

Fill in the required details:

### 1. Demand Note/Requisition Number 
- Auto filled up

### 2. Purchase Order Number
- Auto filled up

### 3. Raised From
- Select whether the request is from **Field** or **Head Office**.

### 4. Cost Center
- Choose the relevant cost center.

### 5. Reference
- Provide a reference identifier.

### 6. Contact
- Add contact details.

### 7. Vendor Number
- Auto filled up

### 8. Terms
- Define terms and conditions.

### 9. Ship Via
- Specify the shipping method.

### 10. Expected Arrival
- Select the expected delivery date.

---

## Order Items

![Order Items](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_order_items.png)

### 1. Condition Field
- Add any conditions related to the order.

### 2. Tax Summary
- Overview of applied taxes.

### 3. Included Tax
- Tax already included in pricing.

### 4. Total Tax
- Total calculated tax amount.

---

## Add Purchase Order Items

![Order Items](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_order_items_additional.png)

Click on **+** button to add items. But the items need to defined in the **Demand Note** list.

Click on **Save** button to save the entered data.

---

## Templates

![Template Page](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_templates.png)

### 1. Template Tab
- Navigate to template section.

### 2. Add Template
- Add new templates if needed.

### 3. Template List
- Displays templates based on **tender category**.

### 4. Vendor Uploaded Documents
- Shows vendor-submitted documents (if available).

---

## Send for Review

![Send for Review Button](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_send_for_review.png)

- Click to send the purchase order for review.

---

## Review Modal

![Review Modal](/img/open_tender/purchase_orders/purchase_order_select_reviewer.png)

### 1. Reviewer Field
- Select reviewer(s).

### 2. Authorizer Field
- Select authorizer(s).

### 3. Send for Review
- Sends the purchase order for approval.

## Summary

This workflow covers:

- Creation of purchase orders  
- Item and tax management  
- Template handling  
- Review and approval process  
- Vendor communication and document handling  

The process ensures a **structured, trackable, and auditable purchase order lifecycle**.
