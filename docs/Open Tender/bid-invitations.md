# Bid Invitations

## Overview

The **Bid Invitations** feature allows procurement managers to send direct invitations to specific bidders to participate in an Open Tender.  

While Open Tenders are by nature open to all qualified bidders, direct invitations can be used to:
- Encourage participation from preferred suppliers
- Ensure awareness among key market players
- Build competition from known capable vendors

All invitation management is accessed through the tender **List Page** or **Edit Page**.

---

## Accessing Invitations

### From Tender List Page

- Click the **Invitation Button** next to a tender to manage bid invitations  

### From Tender Edit Page

- Click the **Invitation Button** on the edit page to open the invitation list  

---

## Invitation List Page

This page displays:

- All bidders invited to the tender  
- Current status of each invited bidder  
- Options to add new bid invitations  
- Quick access to bid evaluation tools

---

## Inviting Bidders

### Step 1: Click Invite Bidder Button

- Click the **Invite Bidder** button to open the invitation modal

### Step 2: Select Bidder or Enter Email

Inside the modal:

1. **Select Bidder or Type Email** – Choose from existing registered bidders or type a new email address
2. **Send Button** – Sends the invitation to the selected bidder(s)

---

## Bidder Status Tracking

The invitations list displays important information:

- **Bidder Details** – Name and contact information
- **Invitation Status** – Whether invitation has been sent
- **Application Status** – Whether bidder has submitted bid
- **Bid Status** – Completeness of submitted bid
- **Evaluation Status** – Whether submitted bid has been evaluated
- **Quick Actions** – Evaluation button for rapid assessment

---

## Key Features

✅ **Optional Invitations**
- Open Tenders don't require direct invitations
- Use invitations strategically to build competition
- All bidders can still participate openly

✅ **Existing Bidders**
- Select from a pre-populated list of registered bidders
- Efficient for repeat suppliers

✅ **New Bidders**
- Type email address to invite new suppliers
- They will receive email invitation
- Automatically created in system upon registration

✅ **Status Tracking**
- Monitor bidder participation in real-time
- Track bid submissions and status
- Quick access to evaluation tools

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Before Inviting:**
- Ensure tender is properly configured and published
- Verify bidder information is accurate
- Consider market competition and balance
- Document invitation rationale for audit trail

⚠️ **Invitation Process:**
- Bidders receive email with invitation link
- They can register if not already in system
- Invitations are transparent and documented
- Maintain records of all invitations sent

ℹ️ **Fairness Considerations:**
- Direct invitations don't restrict open participation
- All interested bidders can still participate
- Document invitation strategy for procurement file
- Ensure no unfair competitive advantages
