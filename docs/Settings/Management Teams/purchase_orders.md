# Purchase Orders

## Accessing Purchase Orders

- Click on **Settings**
- Click on **Management Teams**
- Click on **Purchase Orders**

![Purchase Order Listing](/img/settings/management_teams/purchase_orders/purchase_order_listing.png)

## Listing Members



- Displays all members involved in purchase order evaluation

## Available Actions

- **Team Requirements** – Manage team members for different groups
- **Add Purchase Order Team** – Add new members for purchase order evaluations

## Team Requirements

![Team Requirements](/img/settings/management_teams/purchase_orders/purchase_order_team_requirements.png)

This section allows assigning mimimum number of users to specific groups required for verifying:

- **Head Office Reviewer**
- **Head Office Authorizer**
- **Field Reviewer**
- **Field Authorizer**

### Usage

- Head Office groups are used for verifying purchase orders raised from the head office
- Field groups are used for verifying purchase orders raised from field operations

---

## Add Team Members

- Click on **Add Purchase Order Team**

![Add Purchase Order Team](/img/settings/management_teams/purchase_orders/purchase_order_add_team.png)

- A modal popup appears
- Select users from the dropdown
- After successful addition, the modal automatically closes

## Notes

- Ensure correct users are assigned to appropriate groups
- Team structure directly impacts evaluation and approval workflows
- Users can be updated or removed as needed
