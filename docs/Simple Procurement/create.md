# Create Simple Procurement

This section explains how to create a new Simple Procurement tender in the system.

---

## 📍 Step 1: Navigate to Create Menu

![Create Menu](/img/simple_procurement/simple_procurement_create_menu.png)

- Open the **Simple Procurement** module  
- Click on the **Create Menu** to begin a new tender  

---

## ➕ Step 2: Click Create Button

![Create Button](/img/simple_procurement/simple_procurement_create_button.png)

- Click the **Create** button to open the tender creation form  

---

## 📝 Step 3: Fill Tender Details

![Create Form](/img/simple_procurement/simple_procurement_create_from.png)

Fill in the following fields carefully:

### 🔹 Basic Information

1. **Name**  
   Enter a descriptive name for the procurement

2. **Demand Note**  
   Select the related demand note  

3. **Category**  
   Choose the appropriate procurement category  

4. **Code**  
   Provide a unique tender code for tracking  

---

### 📄 Content & Configuration

5. **Terms and Conditions**  
   Add all applicable terms and conditions

6. **Evaluation Criteria**  
   Select the criteria for evaluating quotations

7. **Template**  
   Choose a document template if required  

---

### 📅 Important Dates

8. **Published Date**  
   9. **Submission Date**  
10. **Opening Date**  

**Important:** Ensure dates are logical:  
- Submission Date > Published Date  
- Opening Date ≥ Submission Date  

---

### 📎 Attachments

11. **Tender Files**  
Upload all required documents and specifications

---

## 💾 Step 4: Save Tender

12. **Save as Draft**  
Click **Draft** to save without publishing  

13. **Publish Tender**  
Click **Save** to publish the tender  

---
