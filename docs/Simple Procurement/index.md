# Simple Procurement

Simple Procurement is designed for low-value procurement processes, typically ranging from **€200 to €9,999**, where efficiency and speed are prioritized while maintaining transparency and accountability.

This module digitizes the full procurement lifecycle — from requesting quotations to evaluation, approval, and contract award — ensuring a structured and traceable workflow within the system.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of Simple Procurement is to:

- Standardize the **Request for Quotation (RFQ)** process  
- Enable structured **quotation evaluation and comparison**  
- Support **digital approval workflows**  
- Facilitate **electronic contract issuance and signing**  
- Ensure **consistency, transparency, and auditability**  

---

## ⚙️ Workflow Overview

The Simple Procurement process follows these key steps:

### 1. 📩 Quotation Request
- RFQ is prepared using a standardized template  
- Sent to a predefined list of vendors  

---

### 2. 📊 Evaluation
- Vendors submit quotations  
- Quotations are compared using a **Comparative Evaluation template**  
- The system helps identify the most competitive offer  

---

### 3. ✅ Approval
- Evaluation results are submitted to the **Core Management Team**  
- Review and approval are completed within the system  

---

### 4. 🏆 Award
- A **Purchase Order (PO)** is typically issued to the selected vendor  
- System allows alternative templates:
  - Service Contract  
  - Works Contract  

- Vendors can **sign and return documents electronically**

## 📌 When to Use Simple Procurement

Use this module when:

- Procurement value is within **€200 – €9,999**  
- Vendors are **pre-identified**  
- A **quick and structured RFQ process** is sufficient  
- Full tendering (open/negotiated) is not required
