# Vendor Invitations

## Overview

The **Vendor Invitations** feature allows procurement managers to invite vendors to participate in a Simple Procurement.  
Vendors can be existing registered users or new ones, who will be prompted to sign up and participate.

All invitation management is accessed through the procurement **List Page** or **Edit Page**.

---

## Accessing Invitations

### From Procurement List Page

![Invitation Button - List Page](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/procurement_list_page_invitation_button.png)

- Click the **Invitation Button** next to a procurement to manage vendor invitations  

### From Procurement Edit Page

![Invitation Button - Edit Page](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/edit_page_invitation_button.png)

- Click the **Invitation Button** on the edit page to open the invitation list  

---

## Invitation List Page

![Invitation List Page](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/invitation_list_page.png)

This page displays:

- All vendors invited to the procurement  
- Current status of each vendor  
- Options to add new vendor invitations  
- Quick access to evaluation tools

---

## Inviting Vendors

### Step 1: Click Invite Vendor Button

![Invite Vendor Button](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/invite_vendor_button.png)

- Click the **Invite Vendor** button to open the invitation modal

### Step 2: Select Vendor or Enter Email

![Vendor Invite Modal](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/vendor_invite_model.png)

Inside the modal:

1. **Select Vendor or Type Email** – Choose from existing registered vendors or type a new email address
2. **Send Button** – Sends the invitation to the selected vendor(s)

---

## Vendor Status Tracking

### Invited Vendors List

![Invited Vendor List](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/invited_vendor_list.png)

The list displays important information:

- **Vendor Details** – Name and contact information
- **Invitation Status** – Whether invitation has been sent
- **Application Status** – Whether vendor has applied/submitted bid
- **Evaluation Status** – Whether submitted bid has been evaluated
- **Quick Actions** – Evaluation button for rapid assessment

---

## Key Features

✅ **Existing Vendors**
- Select from a pre-populated list of registered vendors
- Quick and efficient for repeat suppliers

✅ **New Vendors**
- Type email address to invite new suppliers
- They will receive email invitation to sign up
- Automatically created in the system upon registration

✅ **Status Tracking**
- Monitor vendor participation in real-time
- Track submission progress
- Quick access to evaluations

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Before Inviting:**
- Ensure procurement is properly configured
- Verify vendor information is accurate
- Consider vendor capacity and qualifications

⚠️ **Invitation Process:**
- Vendors receive email with invitation link
- They must register to access the portal
- New vendors have limited time to register
- Resend invitations if no response within reasonable timeframe

ℹ️ **After Invitations Sent:**
- Monitor submission status
- Track vendor responses
- Prepare for bid evaluation
- Document all communications
