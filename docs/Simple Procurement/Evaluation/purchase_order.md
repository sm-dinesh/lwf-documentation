## Access Purchase Order

![Purchase Order Button](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/invitation_listing_page_purchase_order_button.png)

- From the invitation listing page, click this button to navigate to the **Purchase Order** section.

---

## Purchase Order List Page

![Create Button](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_list_page_create_button.png)

- Click the **Create** button to start a new purchase order.

---

## Create Purchase Order

![Create Purchase Order Form](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/create_purchase_order_form.png)

Fill in the required details:

### 1. Raised From
- Select whether the request is from **Field** or **Head Office**.

### 2. Cost Center
- Choose the relevant cost center.

### 3. Reference
- Provide a reference identifier.

### 4. Contact
- Add contact details.

### 5. Terms
- Define terms and conditions.

### 6. Ship Via
- Specify the shipping method.

### 7. Expected Arrival
- Select the expected delivery date.

### 8. Next Button
- Proceeds to the next step.

---

## Order Items

![Order Items](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_order_items.png)

### 1. Order Items Tab
- Displays items included in the purchase order.

### 2. Condition Field
- Add any conditions related to the order.

### 3. Tax Summary
- Overview of applied taxes.

### 4. Included Tax
- Tax already included in pricing.

### 5. Total Tax
- Total calculated tax amount.

### 6. Add Items
- Add items to the purchase order.

### 7. Save Button
- Saves the entered data.

---

## Templates

![Template Page](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_template_page.png)

### 1. Template Tab
- Navigate to template section.

### 2. Add Template
- Add new templates if needed.

### 3. Template List
- Displays templates based on **tender category**.

### 4. Vendor Uploaded Documents
- Shows vendor-submitted documents (if available).

---

## Send for Review

![Send for Review Button](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_send_for_review.png)

- Click to send the purchase order for review.

---

## Review Modal

![Review Modal](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/review_model.png)

### 1. Reviewer Field
- Select reviewer(s).

### 2. Authorizer Field
- Select authorizer(s).

### 3. Send for Review
- Sends the purchase order for approval.

---

## Review Email

![Review Mail](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_review_mail.png)

- Reviewer receives an email notification.
- Click **Review** to open the purchase order.

---

## Approve or Reject

![Approve or Reject](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/approve_or_reject_purchase_order.png)

- Reviewer can:
  - **Approve** the purchase order  
  - **Reject** the purchase order  

---

## Approved Status

![Approved Status](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/purchase_order_approve_status.png)

### 1. Approved Message
- Indicates the purchase order has been approved.

### 2. Mail to Vendor Button
- Sends the purchase order to the vendor.

---

## Vendor Uploaded Documents

![Vendor Uploaded File](/img/simple_procurement/purchase_order/vendor_uploded_file.png)

- After receiving the purchase order, the vendor can upload documents.
- Users can:
  - **View uploaded files**  
  - **Download documents**  

---

## Summary

This workflow covers:

- Creation of purchase orders  
- Item and tax management  
- Template handling  
- Review and approval process  
- Vendor communication and document handling  

The process ensures a **structured, trackable, and auditable purchase order lifecycle**.
