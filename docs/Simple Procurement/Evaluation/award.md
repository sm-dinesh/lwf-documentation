## Invitation & Bids Access

![Invitation Button](/img/simple_procurement/invitations/procurement_list_page_invitation_button.png)

- From the procurement list page, click this button to view **invitations and submitted bids**.

---

## Award Button

![Award Button](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/award_button.png)

- Once the procurement is **approved by the approver**, the **Award** button becomes visible on the invitation page.
- This button is used to award the procurement to a selected bidder.

---

## Award Modal

![Award Modal](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/award_model.png)

Clicking the **Award** button opens a modal with:

### 1. Award Reason
- Provide a reason for awarding the procurement to the selected bidder.

### 2. Submit Button
- Confirms the award.

**Behavior:**
- On submission, the procurement is officially **awarded to the selected bidder**.

---

## Post-Award Actions

After awarding:

![Cancel Award Button](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/cancel_award_button.png)

On the listing page (for the winning bid):

### 1. Purchase Order
- Option to proceed with **purchase order creation**.

### 2. Cancel Award Button
- Allows cancellation of the awarded procurement.

---

## Cancel Award Modal

![Cancel Award Modal](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/cancel_award_button_model.png)

Clicking **Cancel Award** opens:

### 1. Cancel Reason
- Provide a reason for cancelling the award.

### 2. Submit Button
- Confirms the cancellation request.

**Behavior:**
- Submitting triggers an **email notification to the approval team**.

---

## Award Cancellation Email

![Award Cancel Mail](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/award_cancel_mail.png)

- The team receives an email with a **Confirm Cancellation** action.

---

## Award Cancellation Approval

![Cancel Approval Page](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/award_cancel_page_approve.png)

On clicking the confirmation link:

### 1. Approve Button
- Opens approval modal.

### 2. Notes Area
- Optional notes for cancellation approval.

### 3. Approve Button (Final)
- Confirms the cancellation.

---

## Cancellation Status & Next Steps

![Cancel Status on List Page](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/award_cancel_status_on_list_page.png)

After cancellation:

### 1. Award Cancel Status
- Displays status for the cancelled bid.

### 2. Award Approval Button
- Used to initiate awarding for the **next best bidder**.

### 3. Award Approval Team Selection
- Select approval team for the next award process.

---

## Re-Award Process

- After cancellation is finalized, the system automatically:
  - Sends an **email notification** to the next best bidder’s approval team.
  - Initiates the **same approval workflow** as the initial award.

---

## Purchase Order

- Once the award is finalized, the user can proceed to create a **Purchase Order**.
- This process is covered in the next section.

---

## Summary

This workflow ensures:

- Controlled **award and cancellation process**  
- Proper **approval tracking**  
- Automated **email notifications**  
- Seamless transition to the **next best bidder** when needed  

The process maintains full **transparency and auditability** throughout procurement awarding.
