## Evaluation Summary Page

![Evaluation Summary Page Screenshot](/img/simple_procurement/evaluations/evaluaiton_summary_page.png)

This page serves as a **dashboard for evaluating bids**. It is divided into multiple sections, each focusing on a different aspect of the procurement evaluation.

---

### 1. List of Items

Displays all the items included in the procurement:

- **Name/Description** of each item.  
- **Quantity** required.  
- **Unit of measurement** (e.g., pieces, kg, hours).  
- **Unit price** (optional at evaluation stage).  

**Purpose:** Allows evaluators to compare bids accurately against exact requirements.

---

### 2. Special Criteria (VAT)

Indicates which items or bids are eligible for **VAT addition**:

- VAT is applied according to specific rules or criteria.  
- Ensures correct calculation of total costs including taxes.  

**Purpose:** Ensures accurate financial evaluation.

---

### 3. Total in EUR

Displays the **total procurement cost in Euros**:

- Calculated as **sum of eligible items + VAT + other applicable fees**.  
- Provides a quick overview of the financial impact of bids.  

**Purpose:** Helps evaluators quickly compare total costs.

---

### 4. Other Evaluation Criteria

Lists **additional evaluation criteria** beyond price:

- Delivery time  
- Quality certifications  
- Warranty period  
- Environmental compliance  

**Purpose:** Ensures a holistic evaluation, not just based on cost.

---

### 5. Rejection Reasons for Bids

For any bids that **did not meet requirements**, this section provides explanations:

- Missing mandatory documents  
- Price outside allowed range  
- Failure to meet technical specifications  

**Purpose:** Ensures **transparency and accountability**.

---

### 6. List of Bids

Displays all submitted bids:

- **Bidder name** or organization  
- **Submitted amount**  
- **Bid date**  
- Optional: compliance summary with evaluation criteria  

**Purpose:** Enables side-by-side comparison of all bids.

---

### 7. Financial Documents

Allows evaluators to **view or download supporting documents**:

- Quotes, invoices, or bid guarantees  
- Bank statements or financial proofs  
- Bid security documents  

**Purpose:** Verifies financial information before awarding procurement.
