# Send Demand Note for Review

The **Send for Review** feature allows users to submit a Demand Note into the approval workflow. The demand note will be reviewed and approved by designated users in different roles.

## Accessing Send for Review

- Navigate to the Demand Note Edit page
- Click on the **Send for Review** button at the top of the page

## Overview

![Send for Review](/img/demand_note/demand_note_send_for_review.png)

A modal popup will appear where users can select approvers from different roles.

## Approval Roles

The modal contains three levels of approval, indicated in the image:

### 1. Reviewer
- First level of review
- Responsible for initial verification of the demand note

### 2. Checker
- Second level of validation
- Ensures correctness and completeness of the data

### 3. Approver
- Final authority for approval
- Confirms and approves the demand note for further processing

## Sending Invitations

- Select users for each role (Reviewer, Checker, Approver)
- Once selected, submit the form
- Invitations will be sent to the respective users for approval

## Notes

- Ensure correct users are selected for each role before sending
- The approval process follows the defined hierarchy: Reviewer → Checker → Approver
- After submission, editing permissions may be restricted based on workflow settings
