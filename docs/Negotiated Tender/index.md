# Negotiated Tender

Negotiated Tender is designed for specialized procurement processes where direct negotiation with selected suppliers is preferred. This module is typically used for complex projects, professional services, or situations where competition through open tendering is not suitable.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of Negotiated Tender is to:

- Enable **targeted procurement** with pre-selected suppliers
- Support **complex negotiations** for specialized services
- Allow **flexible supplier selection criteria**
- Maintain **transparency** through documentation and audit trails
- Accommodate **unique project requirements**
- Facilitate **relationship-based procurement** when appropriate

---

## ⚙️ Workflow Overview

The Negotiated Tender process follows these key steps:

### 1. 📝 Tender Preparation
- Define scope of work and requirements in detail
- Identify and pre-select appropriate suppliers
- Prepare negotiation framework and evaluation criteria

---

### 2. 💬 Negotiation Phase
- Suppliers are invited directly to participate
- Detailed discussions and negotiations occur
- Proposals are discussed and refined iteratively

---

### 3. 📊 Proposal Evaluation
- Proposals are evaluated against defined criteria
- Negotiations continue based on evaluation findings
- Adjustments and refinements are made as needed

---

### 4. ✅ Award & Contract
- Preferred supplier is selected
- Final contract terms are agreed
- Contract is executed electronically
- Supplier can sign and return documents through the system

## 📌 When to Use Negotiated Tender

Use this module when:

- Procurement requires **specialized expertise** or unique skills
- Only a **limited number of suppliers** are suitable
- **Complex negotiations** are anticipated
- The procurement is for **professional services** or consulting
- **Confidentiality** considerations require selective participation
- Standard competitive tendering is not feasible
